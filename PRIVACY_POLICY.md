# Privacy Policy for Loan Math

**Last Updated:** June 7, 2026

At **Loan Math**, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application, **Loan Math** (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

Loan Math is designed to be a highly functional, secure, and privacy-respecting financial utility. 

### A. Local Calculation & Financial Data (Offline-First)
* **What we collect:** Inputs such as loan amounts, interest rates, durations, additional debt balances, dynamic comparisons, and rent-vs-buy details.
* **How it is used:** All calculation inputs are processed **locally on your device**. None of your mortgage, property values, personal financial inputs, or debt planning details are sent to our servers or stored externally.
* **Data Storage:** Any saved calculations or configurations are stored securely within the App's local sandboxed database (or local preferences) on your device.

### B. User-Initiated Support & Bug Reports
* **What we collect:** Subject line, issue description, and system diagnostic metadata (such as Android device model and OS version).
* **How it is used:** When you choose to report a bug or suggest an improvement within the App, the App uses your local mail client (`mailto:` protocol) to draft an email. You are in full control of reviewing, modifying, and choosing whether or not to send this email. This information is used strictly to resolve technical customer queries and verify software stability.

### C. Device Permissions
The App may request the following permissions during operation:
* **Internet Access (`android.permission.INTERNET`):** Used strictly to support dynamic features, premium verification processes, or load network-based partner assets (where applicable).

## 2. Third-Party Services & Advertisements

Depending on your subscription or plan, the App may integrate third-party developer services of trusted partners:

* **Google Play Services (Diagnostics & Stability):** Google may collect non-identifiable usage statistics, performance analytics, crash logs, and hardware indicators to help us optimize future releases.
* **AdMob (Google Ad Services):** To keep basic features free, the App may display banner or video ads. Google AdMob may collect and process device advertising IDs and location statistics in accordance with Google's dynamic privacy policies to serve context-appropriate ads.
* **In-App Billing (Google Play Billing):** Premium upgrades and simulating premium billing utilize the Google Play Billing framework. Payment transactions and sensitive credit card details are handled directly and securely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details.

For more information, please refer to the privacy policies of our primary processors:
* [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not host a central user database or cloud server for calculation profiles:
* Any local configuration data stored in the App's storage remains on your device for as long as the App is installed.
* You can instantly clear all stored application preferences and calculation histories at any time by clearing the App's storage data via your Android system settings (`Settings > Apps > Loan Math > Storage > Clear Data`).

---

## 4. GDPR Compliance (General Data Protection Regulation)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you are protected by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR. Loan Math is fully committed to compliance with GDPR regulations. Here is how we protect your rights:

### A. Data Minimization & Privacy by Design (Article 25)
Our app is developed utilizing "Privacy by Design" and "Privacy by Default" principles.
* **No Server-side Processing:** We do not collect, upload, share, or store any of your sensitive financial calculation values, budgets, debt entries, or personal preferences on remote servers. All computation resides strictly inside the app's sandboxed local storage on your physical device.
* **No Database Registration:** The App doesn't require user accounts, signup profiles, or remote credentials. 

### B. Access, Portability, and Right to Erasure (Article 17, 20)
* **Access and Portability:** You have complete access to your data because it only lives on your own device. 
* **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You have the absolute right to erase all your local data at any time. You can instantly clear all local databases, preferences, and calculations by uninstalling the application or using your Android system settings (`Settings > Apps > Loan Math > Storage > Clear Data`).

### C. Processing of Support Data
If you initiate support requests or bug reports via email (`mailto:`):
* Any emails you send us are processed solely under the legal basis of **Legitimate Interest** to resolve your customer support queries or fix software bugs.
* We do not use support email addresses for marketing purposes.
* You may contact us at `loopzerotech@gmail.com` at any time to request the deletion of your support email correspondence.
